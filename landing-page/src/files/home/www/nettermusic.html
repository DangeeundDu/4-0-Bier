nettermusic

Create your own playlists with your favorite songs in the netterverse. You can add YouTube urls to a playlist. The urls are to be separated by the | character. You can list your playlists, play a random song from one playlist or you can also delete a playlist. Be careful with the specifier field and only fill it when needed.

Functionality

The following functionality must be retained:

  • It must be possible to create new playlists.
  • It must be possible to delete playlists by id.
  • It must be possible to play a random song from a playlist.
  • It must be possible to list all playlists.
  • Said operations are to be authorized by a username password combination.
