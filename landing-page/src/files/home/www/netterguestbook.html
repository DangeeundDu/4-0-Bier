netterguestbook

This is the most elaborated guestbook on the whole internet. We carefully crafted a unique user experience which is unmatched by any other service. As we consider privacy a valuable human right, we also created the possibility to make secret posts with content, which is not to be seen by the public. However, we highly encourage you to create public posts to keep this guestbook vibrant and interesting.

Functionality

The following functions are supported by this service and must be retained.

  • Create public posts
  • Create secret posts
  • Posts consist of:
    date (String), is_public ("0" xor "1"), secret (String, iff secret post), message (String), image_path (String)
  • Secret posts can be shown by entering the corresponding password.
  • You can also download a PDF of your posts.
  • The core functionalities are therefore creating, showing and downloading which have to work after each attempted fix.
